Menemen

Especially when there is a menemen on the table at the weekend breakfast, that table will be cheered up and beautified.
You can't get enough of the bread and bananas! Although menemen is known as a single or student meal, it can be easily prepared and consumed at any time of the day due to its high nutritional value such as tomatoes, peppers, onions and eggs.
You can't get enough of the taste! Let's prepare a delicious breakfast menemen that you can enjoy at any time of the day.
Introducing: Tomato pepper onion menemen recipe! Of course, you can also prepare it without onions.

Recipe: Poyraz Kavak

For How Many Person: For 3 person. Preparation Time: 5 minutes. Cooking Time: 15 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 piece onion
  • 4 green peppers
  • 1 capia pepper
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 sprig of spring onion
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tea spoon salt
  • 1 tea spoon black pepper

How To Do Menemen With Onion?

  1. Take the olive oil in a deep pan with a non-stick bottom and fry the finely chopped onions until they turn pink.
  2. Then add the green peppers and capia peppers and continue the roasting process.
  3. When the peppers start to wilt, add the chopped tomatoes, close the lid and cook until the tomatoes are soft, about 10 minutes will be enough.
  4. Finally, add the finely chopped spring onions and when the onions turn pale, break the eggs and mix them.
  5. Stir until the eggs are cooked, then turn off the heat and serve hot. Enjoy your meal!
